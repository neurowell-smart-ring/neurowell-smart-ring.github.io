Neurowell – AI Smart Ring

A revolutionary wearable that understands your body and mind together.

About Neurowell

Neurowell is a pioneering AI-enabled smart ring designed to deliver the first truly holistic view of human health by concurrently tracking both physiological vitals and cognitive state markers. Traditional wearables frequently misinterpret the body’s signals, often conflating cardiovascular responses caused by physical exertion with those triggered by mental stress or emotional overload. Neurowell overcomes this limitation through its intelligent dual-sensor architecture.

Equipped with medical-grade PPG sensors for precise cardiovascular monitoring and advanced EDA electrodes for measuring electrodermal activity—a direct indicator of sympathetic nervous system arousal—Neurowell captures both physical and emotional health cues with exceptional accuracy. Its proprietary AI engine analyzes heart rate patterns, sweat gland activity, motion signals, and situational context to distinguish between “good stress,” such as exercise, and “bad stress,” such as anxiety or cognitive fatigue.

When elevated mental stress is detected, the ring initiates a real-time biofeedback loop using subtle haptic alerts. This directs users to scientifically guided stress-relief exercises on the companion app, enabling proactive management of mental and emotional strain. Compact, durable, and discreet, Neurowell is more than a fitness tracker—it is an all-in-one health companion engineered for modern well-being.

Features

Real-Time Cognitive Stress Detection

Utilizing integrated Electrodermal Activity (EDA) sensors to measure skin conductance changes, providing an objective, real-time biomarker for mental load and emotional stress.

Advanced Physical Health Monitoring

Comprehensive tracking of foundational physical metrics, including Heart Rate, Heart Rate Variability (HRV), blood oxygen (SpO2), sleep stages, and activity levels via optical and motion sensors.

AI-Driven "Dual-Mode" Analysis Engine

A proprietary machine learning system that fuses multivariate sensor data to accurately differentiate between physiological arousal caused by physical exertion ("good stress") versus cognitive strain ("bad stress").

Proactive Biofeedback Loop

A closed-loop system that not only detects elevated cognitive stress but immediately provides discreet haptic alerts on the ring and guides the user through real-time interventions to reduce mental fatigue.

Methodology

Neurowell’s AI-driven architecture combines multi-sensor data processing, EDA-based emotional analytics, cardiovascular modeling, motion interpretation, and a closed-loop intervention engine to create a unified health intelligence system.
